4.2.2 Board Meetings Quorum consists of 2/3 of the Board of Directors or five Board members, whichever is greater.

[As amended at the General Meeting of November 21, 1991] 4.2.3 Procedure 4.2.3.1 Election of Officers The election of officers shall be by secret ballot. A simple majority of those casting votes (including abstaining votes) is required.

If on the first ballot no candidate receives a simple majority, then there will be a runoff between the top two candidates. The voting will continue until one candidate receives a simple majority. 4.2.3.2 Other Votes All other votes require a simple majority to pass. 4.2.3.3 Proxy An OCF Member must be present at a meeting to vote.
