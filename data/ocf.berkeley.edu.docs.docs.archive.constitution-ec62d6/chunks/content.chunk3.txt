It is also the intent of this group to appeal to all members of the Berkeley campus community with unsatisfied computing needs and to provide a place for those interested in computing to fully explore that interest.

It is the intention of this group that no small number of people ever control the accessibility of any OCF sponsored computing facility.

Articles 1 Name This organization shall be called the Open Computing Facility and may also be referred to as the OCF .

2 Members 2.1 General Membership Any UC Berkeley student, faculty or staff member may be an OCF Member.

2.2 Active Members 2.2.1 Eligibility Only currently registered students, faculty, and staff may be active members in a registered student organization. Only active members may vote or hold office. [As amended at the General Meeting of September 1, 2015, with effect from June 26, 2015.]
