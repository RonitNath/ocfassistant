In order to do so, you will need to add the ocfweb dev config to your Staff VM's Puppet configuration, so Puppet can install ocfweb 's config files.

To do this, clone the puppet repo or go to /opt/puppet/env/<you> on lightning , and then in hieradata/nodes/ add the following lines to your staff VM's Hiera configuration: classes: - ocf_ocfweb::dev_config Take a look at the configs for fireball or raptors for examples.
