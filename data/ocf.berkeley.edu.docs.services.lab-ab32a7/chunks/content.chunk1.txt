They are maintained by OCF volunteer staff who operate the servers, workstations, and networking gear that helps the lab run. The computer lab contains 3 scanners, 3 standing desks, and a Blu-ray/DVD/CD reader/writer.

OCF computers include free and open-source software such as LibreOffice (similar to Microsoft Office), GIMP (similar to Adobe Photoshop), and more. For details about printing in the lab, see Printing . Location The OCF computer lab is located in 171 MLK Student Union.
