If the violation of OCF policies is additionally a violation of the UC Berkeley Student Code of Conduct, the GM will submit a report to the Center of Student Conduct.

If the user is banned, they shall be promptly notified of the ban and given a chance to appeal the ban at one of the next three future Board of Directors meetings. At that meeting, they will be asked to explain their actions.

The Board of Directors then may decide with a ⅔ majority vote by secret ballot to uphold the ban; if the ban is not upheld, it shall be rescinded.
